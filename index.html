Usage

1. Tap Start new hole to begin a hole (your first tap starts the round).

2. Hit your shot, then tap Log Shot where you stand.

3. Walk to your ball. Hit again. Tap Log Shot.

4. Repeat to the green. Tap Start new hole after logging the hole-out putt.

5. Tap Finish round when the round is complete.
